We may share your data with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, email) when required for website operation or service delivery. We require appropriate security measures and compliance from these providers.

8. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, object, and data portability. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@digitalac.rs.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@digitalac.rs.
